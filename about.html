About

Hey! I'm Ro Saravana, the creator of Mooving Around. I created this site for Hack Davis 2025, my final hackathon as an undergrad. Davis has such a diverse set of viable transportation options, so I thought it would be cool to compile a *somewhat* complete list of transportation abouts available to students.

I had a lot of opinions to share as well so I tried to put my input for things I do or don't recommend based on my experiences.
