Commuting via Amtrak

I know some people commute from Sacramento to Davis via Amtrak. It's important to know that Amtrak often experiences signficant delays and is pretty hit or miss on whether it is going to show up on time. If I was on a strict timeline, I do not think I would take the train.

Long Trips via Amtrak

I think Amtrak is at its best when you are going on a long trip and are not in a rush. The routes are often very scenic and tranquil. A long Amtrak trip is a great time for you to get some work done, meet new people, or just relax and enjoy the scenery.
